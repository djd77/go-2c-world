Museu do Catetinho

The president's first official residence in Brasilia. The building was designed by Oscar Niemeyer, who designed many other buildings within the city. Construction was completed in only 10 days

Location

  • Highway BR-040
  • South Exit
  • Km 0 Gama
  • DF

Main Features

  • Open 9am to 5pm, Tuesday to Sunday
  • Free Parking
  • Toilets available
  • Bring your own food and drinks

Árvore dos Desejos

Two Italian Ficus trees, planted in 1963 by Lourivaldo Soares Marques to provide shelter to his newsstand (Which was the first newsstand in the city). The trunks have formed a tunnel-like passage which locals believe will make their wishes come true if they are made while passing through the tunnel.

Location

  • SHCS SQS 108
  • Asa Sul
  • Brasília
  • DF 70390-100

Main Features

  • Newsstand open 8am to 6pm every day
  • Food and drinks available
  • Dvd rental
  • Newspapers and books available
  • Internet access
  • Printing and laminating services
